High

See bKash [route.ts (line 4)](D:/beyond seo/chatminsahbeautyrealtime/minsahcodexgpt/app/api/payments/bkash/create/route.ts:4) and card [route.ts (line 4)](D:/beyond seo/chatminsahbeautyrealtime/minsahcodexgpt/app/api/payments/card/create/route.ts:4).

Examples: [orders (line 33)](D:/beyond seo/chatminsahbeautyrealtime/minsahcodexgpt/app/api/admin/orders/route.ts:33), [order patch/delete (line 192)](D:/beyond seo/chatminsahbeautyrealtime/minsahcodexgpt/app/api/admin/orders/[id]/route.ts:192), [customers (line 10)](D:/beyond seo/chatminsahbeautyrealtime/minsahcodexgpt/app/api/admin/customers/route.ts:10).
